About Us

Welcome to our website. GATES Institute of Technology, established in 2001 is approved by AICTE and has permanent affiliation to JNTUA. GATES Institute is started with a commitment to bring quality technical education opportunity to the door steps of the youth of backward region of Rayalaseema. GATES will be a home away from home for those in pursuit of excellence. Students here, are encouraged in this journey by our committed faculty and world class facilities. Our faculty provide consistent, high-quality and appropriate academic advising, monitoring, mentoring, nurturing and coaching to ensure academic and personal success of all our students. Our belief in value based education and the service activities we take up moulds our students into finest and responsible human beings. Thanks for your interest in knowing about us. V.K.Padmavathamma Correspondent
